KajuKatli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • water
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder

Process

  1. Powder Cashews: Blend dry cashews into a fine powder without releasing oil.
  2. Make Sugar Syrup: Heat sugar and water until one-string consistency forms.
  3. Mix Cashew Powder: Stir in cashew powder with syrup until thick and non-sticky.
  4. Knead Dough: Knead the warm mixture into a smooth dough with greased hands.
  5. Roll Dough: Roll dough thinly between parchment sheets and Slice rolled dough into diamond shapes.
  6. Set and Store: Let pieces set, then store in an airtight container and Enjoy!!!

Burfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Nuts

Process

  1. Melt Ghee: Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Mix Milk & Sugar: Add milk and sugar, stir until dissolved.
  3. Add Milk Powder: Stir in milk powder to make a lump-free mix.
  4. Cook: Cook until thick and non-sticky.
  5. Flavor: Add cardamom powder.
  6. Set: Transfer to a greased tray and flatten.
  7. Garnish: Add nuts and press lightly.
  8. Cut & Serve: Cool, cut into pieces, and enjoy!!!
